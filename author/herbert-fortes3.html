My packages

Bye Bye Duc

The Debian package received a request for a feature. The
guy was in a rush, with bad behavior (my opinion) and the
way he wanted to fix his needs was not a good one. He knew
about a better option. An option suggested by the Policy.

I asked for an opinion on debian-mentors. And there the
important thing was to choose the solution to the request.

After some useless emails someone point to the 'alternatives'
solution - the Policy choice. And I decided to stop. The
good solution was clear.

I assume my lousy humor with the issue. The issue was not a
big problem (to me), the guy preferred the 'conflicts package'
solution and he was in a hurry.

To see with kind eyes, I decided that the software is too much
important to the guy and he needed a fast solution. So I made
the package orphan and said he could assume the maintenance of
the package. Since October I do not have the software installed
anyway.

Not everybody understood I guess. But I believe nobody is feeling
bad about the issue.
